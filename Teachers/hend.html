About Ms. Hend

Ms. Hend is our innovative computer science teacher who makes technology accessible and fun for all students. With a background in software engineering and a master's degree in Computer Education, she brings real-world experience into the classroom.

Before joining Anas bn Malek School, Ms. Hend worked as a developer at a leading tech company, giving her practical insights that she shares with her students. Her passion for coding and problem-solving is contagious!

Teaching Approach

Ms. Hend believes in a hands-on, project-based approach to teaching computer science. Her classes are interactive workshops where students learn by doing. She emphasizes creative thinking and practical applications of technology.

Achievements

  • Established the school's first coding club and annual hackathon
  • Mentored students who won national coding competitions
  • Developed a custom curriculum that introduces programming concepts to beginners
  • Certified instructor for multiple programming languages and platforms
