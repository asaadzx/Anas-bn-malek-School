About Ms. Sara

Ms. Sara has been teaching English at Anas bn Malek School for over 5 years. She graduated with honors from Cairo University with a degree in English Literature and has a TEFL certification.

Her teaching philosophy centers around creating an immersive English environment where students can practice and develop their skills naturally. She believes that language learning should be fun and engaging, which is why her classes often include games, role-plays, and multimedia elements.

Teaching Approach

Ms. Sara uses a communicative approach to language teaching, focusing on practical usage rather than just grammar rules. Her students consistently show improvement in their English proficiency, with many going on to excel in international English exams.

Achievements

  • Led the school's English debate team to regional championships twice
  • Developed an innovative English learning program for struggling students
  • Recipient of the "Teacher of the Year" award in 2023
  • Published author of educational English materials for young learners
