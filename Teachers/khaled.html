About Mr. Khaled

Mr. Khaled is our beloved Arabic language teacher who instills in students not only linguistic skills but also important values of patience and responsibility. His deep knowledge of Arabic literature and grammar is matched by his ability to connect with students on a personal level.

With a background in classical Arabic studies and modern teaching methodologies, Mr. Khaled bridges tradition and innovation in his classroom. He is known for his calm demeanor and encouraging teaching style that helps students build confidence in their language abilities.

Teaching Approach

Mr. Khaled believes that language learning should be immersive and culturally rich. His lessons incorporate Arabic poetry, stories, and media to create a comprehensive language experience. He emphasizes both written and spoken Arabic, ensuring students develop well-rounded skills.

Achievements

  • Developed a progressive Arabic curriculum that has been adopted by other schools
  • Founded the school's Arabic literary magazine, showcasing student writing
  • Mentored students who have won national Arabic writing competitions
  • Certified in advanced teaching methodologies for second language acquisition
